Instructions for Online Test

Please read the instructions carefully before starting the test.

  1. Click start on bottom of your screen to begin the test.
  2. Click one of the answer, simply click the desired option button.
  3. Candidate will be allowed to Shuffle between questions anytime duing the examination as per their convenience.
  4. All the answered questions will be counted for calculating the final score.
  5. Do not click final SUBMIT on the left corner of the screen nless you have completed the exam.In case you click final SUBMIT you will not be permitted to continue.
  6. Score obtained will be displayed immediately after the test.
